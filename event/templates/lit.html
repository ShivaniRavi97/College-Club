LITERATURE


1.OPEN MIC (poetry, storytelling,stand-up comedy)
In this event each participant has to be prepared to recite or speak their original piece A time interval of 5 minutes will be given to each participant. No restriction on topic selection.The pieces must be in English. Winner will be selected on the basis on originality of content, delivering and connecting to the audience. The pieces must be submitted in print to the coordi￾nators 15 minutes before the start of the event.Pla￾giarism or vulgarity(Stand-up) will lead to disquali￾fication.
2.DEBATE
This event consists of two rounds: Round 1: For the first round the topic will be given before-hand and each participant would have to speak for 2 mins for or against the topic. Time will be given for rebuttals. Round 2: In second round,topic will be given on￾spot and participants will be randomly organised into for and against teams. The members have to speak depending on the team they are in.The team that survives longest in terms of logics and approaches to prove themselves right will be deemed the winner. Winner will be based on both individual and group performances.
3.WRITING COMPETITION
It can be an essay, tale or poem. In this competition we will provide a word, phrase, situation or an ending Everyone will then be given maximum 35 mins to write their piece.
