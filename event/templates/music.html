MUSIC


1.CLASSICAL MUSIC(vocals):
The contestants can sing anything from the Indian classical genre (Carnatic or Hindustani) The time given will be 8 minutes. A warning bell will be given after 6 minutes and the contestants have to finish within 2 minutes after the warning bell. The participants can use shruti box (Tanpura) or any other (just one) classical rhythmic instrument￾for accompanying them.
2.VOICE OF CSE:
The participants may sing any film hits/album songs or any song from western genre. The total amount of time given will be 7 minutes. A warning bell will be give after 5 mins and the contestants have to finish within 2 minutes after the warning bell The contestants can use a karaoke track(Strictly without backup vocals) or can be accompanied by any TWO instruments. 3.War Of Instruments The participants can play any instrument of their choice in any genre.
